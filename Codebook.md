# Codebook

## Subject and Activity

1. subjects ```numbers 1 through 30 represent subject IDs```
    <br /> type: factors
    <br /> 30 levels:  1-30
    
2. activities ```6 kinds of activities recorded by the device``` 
    <br />type: factors
    <br />6 levels:   
    <br />1 = WALKING
    <br />2 = WALKING_UPSTAIRS
    <br />3 = WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
    <br />4 = SITTING
    <br />5 = STANDING
    <br />6 = LAYING
